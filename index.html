About AURORA

AURORA is a multi-layered research project focused on developing quantum-enhanced oversight and governance frameworks for intelligent operating systems and AI-driven environments. The project addresses emerging cybersecurity and digital safety challenges, including deepfake misuse, AI-driven financial fraud, systemic vulnerabilities, and threats targeting government digital infrastructures.
