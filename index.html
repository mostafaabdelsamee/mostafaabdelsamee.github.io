About AURORA

AURORA is a multidisciplinary research project focused on building quantum-enhanced, multi-layered oversight architectures for advanced AI ecosystems. It aims to address security, governance, digital autonomy risks, AI-driven cyberattacks, and responsible deployment of powerful intelligent systems.
