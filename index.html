About AURORA

AURORA is a comprehensive research project exploring advanced AI governance, digital oversight, and quantum-assisted threat classification for intelligent operating environments. The project addresses emerging global risks driven by AI misuse, including deepfake-enabled fraud, automated intrusion, systemic vulnerabilities, and digitally enhanced attacks targeting banking systems, government infrastructures, and sensitive digital networks.

The research also introduces the bio-digital relational model, emphasizing collaborative interaction between human researchers and AI systems for enhanced security, transparency, and resilience.
